Real Estate Services

Land is unique as it is immovable and thus becomes a valuable asset. Land ownership is defined by access to a land title. In India land ownership is acquired after a registered sale deed. Other documents needed are record of rights, property tax receipts and survey documents. Land records consist of various types of information (property area, sale deed etc.) Poor maintenance of land record affects future property transactions as well. The government has implemented National Land Record Modernization Program in order to improve quality of land records.
